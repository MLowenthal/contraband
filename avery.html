Avery "Beaver" Suter

President, Cutter/Handler

The man that keeps the team moving, Avery Suter is a native of Connecticut. As President Avery is responsible for many of the logisitics that allow Contraband to function as team: From Jersey Design to Tournament transport and registration. No one doubts Avery's logisitical prowess and determination to ensure Contraband's continued sucess.

On the field, many compare Avery to Contraband's captain Robert Hsu in terms of his true versitility. Making the switch from Handler to Cutter when needed, Avery is one member of Contraband that is impossible to miss. Serving as one of the most dependable overall players on the team's roster, Avery's understanding of the game and drive to win make him a player that other teams must take note of if they plan to test their luck against Contraband.
