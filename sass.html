Ben "Bus" Sass

Captain & Handler

Hailing from Cleveland, OH Ben Sass brings a level of dedication to the game that few can rival. Notorious for always bidding for the disc, catchable or not, Ben is a constant reminder of what hardwork can do in sports.

Serving as a contrast to his fellow captain, Ben focuses more on the pump-up and energy aspects of the game. His constant volume and energy translate onto the field to create a rare team dynamic with any line he is a part of. Ben is truly a player that anyone can trust to give it his all for every point he plays.
